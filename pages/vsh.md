#### Motivation

Vector Spherical Harmonics (VSH) are the special case of Tensor Spherical Harmonics appropriate for eigenmode decomposition in spatial 3D, notably for systems amenable to separation of poloidal and toroidal components.  

An installable, open-source Fortran package for all manner of essential VSH computations are found in the [Github repo](https://github.com/justinelfritz/FORTVSH). Please review the corresponding whitepaper (available soon) for technical context.
