#### Motivation

Vector Spherical Harmonics (VSH) are the subclass of Tensor Spherical Harmonics appropriate for eigenmode decomposition in spatial 3D, especially for systems amenable to separation of poloidal and toroidal components.  

Fortran code for all manner of essential VSH computations are found in my [Github repo](https://github.com/justinelfritz/fortran_VSH). Please review the corresponding whitepaper (PDF coming soon) for technical context.
